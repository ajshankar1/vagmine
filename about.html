Our Story

Built on Decades of Craft

Vagmine Overseas Pvt. Ltd. is a third-generation rubber compound manufacturer based in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. Founded in 2010, our leadership brings over 40 years of combined industry expertise to every batch we produce.

From our state-of-the-art SIPCOT facility in Gangaikondan, we manufacture and supply FRRC, FRC, Granulated Friction Rubber, Tread Compounds, and Industrial Seals to premier tyre brands globally. Our North American operations are managed through our subsidiary office in Delaware, USA.

We are proud to serve some of the world's most respected names — Yokohama-ATG, BKT Tyres, Nexen Tyre, and Sundaram Industries (TVS Group) — with consistent, specification-grade rubber compounds delivered on time, every time.
